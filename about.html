About Us

We love picking mushrooms in the forest! In some countries mushroom hunting is very popular. Identification does take practice and so does finding the mushrooms. Most mushrooms need very specific conditions. For example, some mushrooms only grow under certain types of trees . Only go hunting with an experienced mushroom hunter ... as in the Czech adage goes: "All mushrooms are edible ... but some only once!"
